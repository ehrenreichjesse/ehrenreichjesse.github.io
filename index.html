About Me

Welcome to my website. I am an aerospace engineering honors student at the University of Maryland with a strong interest in mechanical, electrical, and computational systems. Creative work like this has strengthened my attention to detail and the ability to iterate on ideas, which translates well into technical problem solving skills. I also have significant experience working in school environments where I developed strong communication, adaptability, and problem-solving skills while helping students engage with their learning.
